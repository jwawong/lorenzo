### Session 1: Core & Back Armor (Gym Edition)
**Date:** 2026-02-17
**Duration:** 60 Minutes
**Focus:** Anti-Extension, Anti-Rotation, Lower Back Health
**Equipment:** Cables, Back Extension Machine, Bulgarian Bag, Weights

**1. Warm-Up (10 Mins) - Mobility**
*   Cat-Cow Stretch: 2 mins
*   Cable Face Pulls (Light): 2 sets x 15 reps (Posture/Upper Back)
*   Bulgarian Bag Rotational Swings (Very Light): 1 min (Wake up the spine)

**2. The "Bulletproof" Strength Block (20 Mins) - Stability**
*   *Rest 60s between sets.*
*   **Cable Pallof Press:** 3 sets x 12 reps per side (Anti-rotation standard)
*   **Weighted Back Extensions (Roman Chair):** 3 sets x 12 reps (Hold plate to chest. Squeeze glutes at top)
*   **Hanging Leg Raises (Captains Chair):** 3 sets x 10 reps (Control the eccentric/down phase)

**3. The "Power" Sculpting Block (20 Mins) - Dynamic**
*   *Rest 60s between sets.*
*   **Bulgarian Bag Spin (or Halo):** 3 sets x 10 reps per direction (Amazing for core torque)
*   **Cable Woodchoppers (High to Low):** 3 sets x 12 reps per side
*   **Weighted Plank:** 3 sets x 45 sec (Place plate on lower back)

**4. The Finisher (10 Mins) - Endurance**
*   **Farmers Carry (Heavy Dumbbells/Kettlebells):** 3 walks x 30 meters
    *   *Why:* Forces perfect posture under load. Best posture fixer.

**5. Cool Down (5 Mins)**
*   Dead Hang (Pull-up bar): 1 min (Decompresses spine)
*   Cobra Stretch: 1 min
